What is LSS?

LSS stands for "lagen om st√∂d och service" and it translates to "the law of support and service". This law is made for people who have special needs. The purpose of the law, is to make sure that the society provide people with disabilities and special needs with the right kind of support they need in order to become and feel like normal people. For example, according to LSS, a person with disabilties might have access to support like:

  • Personal assistance with things that has to do with everyday life
  • A place to live with integrated support for people with special needs.
  • Escort services outside the own home.
The list with different kind of support according to LSS is long. What all of them have in common, is to make life easier and, if possible, more fun for someone with disabilities. The goal is also to make them as selfsufficient as possiple.

How to get help according to LSS

In order to get help according to LSS, you have to live in Sweden. You then have to send a written application to the swedish county where you are a resident. You should be able to confirm your needs together with the application. It can for example be a doctors opinion. A case manager will then make an assessment of your application. It is the county where you live that is responsible for providing you with information about LSS.

An important note to make

It is important to note that not all disabled people can recieve help according to LSS. The complete description of the law is "the law of support and service for some disabled people." The case manager will assess if you are one of those who can recieve help and support according to LSS.
