What is F.K?

F.K, is shorthand for Försäkringskassan. That is an insitution in Sweden which is part of the social support system. F.K is where you go when you need financial support if you are unable to support yourself financially. The reasons for why someone can not support themselfs can be many, but F.K is supposed to help you financially if you are in need for it.

How to get help from F.K

In order to get help from F.K, you first need to see a doctor. He or she will determine your condition and give you a written certificate where his or shes opinions are stated. If the doctors opinion is that you are unable to work, then F.K should relate to that. That is why you need a written certificate from a doctor in order to get help from F.K.

F.K is not a popular institution

It is worth mentioning that F.K may have their own opinion about your physical and/or mental condition. That means, that even if you have a statement from a doctor, which clearly indicates that you are unable to work, F.K can still deny you finacial support. That is also the main reason why F.K is not a popular institution, and has not been for many years.

An important note to make

Another thing that is important to note, is that you have to live in Sweden in order to get help from F.K. However, what it means to actually live in Sweden, is F.K the one to decide.
