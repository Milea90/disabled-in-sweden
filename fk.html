What is FK?


FK, is shorthand for Försäkringskassan. That is an insitution in Sweden which is part of the social support system. FK is where you go when you need financial help when you can not support yourself. Maybe you have been in an accident and are unable to work. Maybe you have got a bad disease that prevents you from working. The reasons for why one can not support themselfs can be many, but FK is supposed to help you financially when you are in need for it.



How to get help from FK


In order to get help from FK, you first need to see a doctor. He or she will determine your condition and give you a written certificate where his or shes opinions are stated. If the doctors opinion is that you are unable to work, then FK should atend to thät. That is why you need the written certificate from the doctor in order to get help from FK.



FK is not a popular institution


It is worth mentioning that FK may have their own opinion about your physical and/or mental condition. That means, that even if you have a statement from the doctor which clearly indicates that you are unable to work, FK can still deny you help. That is also the main reason why FK is a very impopular institution.



An important note to make


Another thing that is important to note, is that you have to live in Sweden in order to get help from FK. What it means to actually live in Sweden is FK the one to decide.
