What is the view on disabilities is Sweden?

If you are planning to move to Sweden as a disabled person, you might have some concerns regarding the view on disabilities in Sweden. In order for you to feel safe and normal where you are, you have to be treated fairly. But it is almost impossible to know such things in advance. Therefore, you will get a hint of what it might be like if you decide to move to Sweden.

Swedes are tolerant

The first thing you need to know about swedes, is that they are a very tolerable people. They have a high acceptance for different religions, different sexual orientations and also a very high acceptance for differencies over all. A normal swede will not look twice at someone in a wheelchair for example. However, there will always be idiots everywhere you go so there are no garanties for not being misstreated.

Sweden has a lot of support to offer

One reason why swedes may have such a high tolerance for disabled people, might be because there is a lot of support in place for people with disabilities. The general rule of thumb, is that a person with disabilities should be able to live as normal as possible and be as self sufficient as possible with a bit of help from the society.
