What is D.O

D.O is shorthand for "Diskrimineringsombudsmannen" which is an administrative authority in Sweden. It handles legal cases where people have been discriminated by the society. In Sweden, it is illigal to discriminate and treat people with disabilities different from other people. Therefore, D.O is there to persue legal cases on the behalf of those disabled people who might have been discriminated.

Important to note, is that D.O does not persue legal cases between private individuals. They only handle cases where corporations, institutions and such are involed.

How to get help from D.O

In order to persue a legal case with help from D.O, you have to write to D.O and describe in detail what has happened. Describe when it happened, where it happened and who was involved. D.O will then assess your information and decide wether or not they will persue a legal case based on your information.
