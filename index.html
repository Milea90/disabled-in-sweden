Welcome!

This website is for those with disabilities who just moved, or are planning to move to Sweden. What kind of support is there for disabled in Sweden and how do you get it? We will cover the basics in this website.

It can be a struggle to access this kind of information if you do not know where to look. It can also be difficult to understand it once you find it. That is why this website will provide you with overall, basic and gathered information about the subject.

A summary of what this website will cover

In this website, you can find information about the social support system in Sweden, called "L.S.S". You will also find information about the financial support system, called "F.K". Apart from that, you can find out how you can use "D.O" to sue someone who has misstreated you because of your disabilities. Use the navigation bar at the top of the page to navigate to the different topics. If you are interested, you can read about the view on disabled people in Sweden. Click on "Learn more" in the navigation bar to read more about it.
