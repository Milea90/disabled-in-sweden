Welcome

On this website, you will find basic information about the support systems in Sweden, especially for those with disabilities. It can be a struggle to find that kind of information. It can also be difficult to understand it. That is why this website will provide you with overall and basic information about the subject.

A summary of what this website will cover

In this website, you can find information about the social support system in Sweden, called "LSS". You will also find information about the financial support system, called "FK". Apart from that, you can find out how you can use "DO" to sue someone who has misstreated you because of your disabilities. Use the navigationbar at the top of the page to navigate to the different topics. If you are interested, you can read about the view on disabled people in Sweden. Click on "Learn more" in the navigationbar to read more about that.
