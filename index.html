Welcome

Are you planning to move to Sweden? If so, you should know what kind of support you can recieve. That sort of information is crucial to know if you are a person with disabilities and rely on support to live a normal life.

On this website, you will find basic information about the support systems in Sweden, especially for those with disabilities. It can be a struggle to access that kind of information if you do not know where to look. It can also be difficult to understand it once you find it. That is why this website will provide you with overall and basic information about the subject.

A summary of what this website will cover

In this website, you can find information about the social support system in Sweden, called "LSS". You will also find information about the financial support system, called "FK". Apart from that, you can find out how you can use "DO" to sue someone who has misstreated you because of your disabilities. Use the navigation bar at the top of the page to navigate to the different topics. If you are interested, you can read about the view on disabled people in Sweden. Click on "Learn more" in the navigation bar to read more about that.
